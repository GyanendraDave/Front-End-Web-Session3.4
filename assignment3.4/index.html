Diana goes up against the Soviets in ‘Wonder Woman 2

‘Wonder Woman 2’ will be a period piece, with Princess Diana facing off against the Soviet Union during the 1980s.

DC Entertainment President Geoff Johns is currently developing the script of the sequel to ‘Wonder Woman’ with director Patty Jenkins, reported The Wrap.

Chris Pine, who played Steve Trevor in ‘Wonder Woman’, is reportedly optioned by Warner Brothers to appear in the sequel.

‘Wonder Woman’ that released on June 23 has earned USD 16.9 million worldwide till now.
